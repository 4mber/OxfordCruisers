Oxford Cruisers Ltd.


Who we are...


Oxford Cruisers Ltd. is a family run working boatyard, situated on the Thames, in the heart of Oxfordshire, UK. With over 28 years of experience, we specialise in the restoration, maintenance and repair of Narrowboats, Dutch Barges, and Cruisers. From small routine jobs to ambitious projects, we offer a wide range of professional marine services.
